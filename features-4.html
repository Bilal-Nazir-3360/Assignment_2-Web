Detailed Description

Our deadline alert system ensures you never miss an important task or project deadline. With intelligent notification scheduling and customizable reminder settings, you can stay on top of your work without feeling overwhelmed.

Features include:
  • Smart notification scheduling
  • Priority-based alerts
  • Multiple notification channels
  • Customizable reminder intervals
  • Do Not Disturb settings
  • Notification history and tracking
